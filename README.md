# Literature-search-using-SAS-programming

Using SAS programming (https://github.com/samalkani/Literature-search-using-SAS-programming/blob/main/Literature%20Search_inprogress.sas) to organize and categorize a list of research topics from excel spreadsheet (https://github.com/samalkani/Literature-search-using-SAS-programming/blob/main/Literature%20search%20-%20complete%20set.xlsx) composed of 130 scientific research papers obtained by a literaure search on Pubmed on the broad area of  type 2 diabetes and cardiometabolic disease so that research 'gaps' can be identified for future research in the form of a PhD proposal
